01 / About

The Person
Behind the Screen

I'm a Lagos-based creative technologist who started with CorelDraw and ended up writing code, wrangling data, and automating workflows with AI.

My journey began in 2018 as a self-taught graphic designer, driven by curiosity and a hunger to turn ideas into visual experiences. By 2020 I was taking on real client work — brand identities, print media, flyers — out of local studios across Lagos.

In 2023 I made the leap into front-end development and it clicked immediately. My design background meant I wasn't just writing functional code — I was building things that felt good. Today I bridge three worlds: interactive UI engineering, brand design, and data analytics — all under one creative roof.

Currently pursuing a BSc in Statistics at Olabisi Onabanjo University, deepening my analytical edge with SPSS, R, and predictive modelling to complement my engineering and design toolkit.
